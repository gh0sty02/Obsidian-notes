When I was looking for a job switch in 2020, I worked so hard + strategically with coding that eventually in 45 days, I became really good. I cracked coding interviews at Google, Apple, Amazon before Covid Hit and all further interviews were canceled. I eventually cleared another coding interview at Cedar and started working there and eventually joined PayPal. This 45 days roadmap with 130+ problems has served me really well and its also helped students land jobs.

Sharing the day by day details and if you start today, by May 31, you will be a DSA Champion.

## Printable Calendar: Link

STRATEGY OVERVIEW:

How to solve.

1. Take these three problems and give them 20 minutes each - set a timer.
2. If you are not able to solve any problem, take a look at solution and understand what went wrong
3. Watch video explanations if needed.
4. Read and keep thinking about it.
5. At night, give yourself 30 minutes. 10 mins per problem.
6. And try to solve it again. If you did it, great, if not try again next day.
