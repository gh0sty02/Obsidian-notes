Denmark is one of the most immigrant-friendly job markets in Europe — strong salaries, excellent work-life balance, and a government that actively recruits international talent to fill skilled labor shortages.

If you’re looking to break into the Danish job market, here’s exactly where to look and who’s hiring.

---

**🌐 Job Portals That Welcome International Candidates**

**[WorkInDenmark](https://www.workindenmark.dk/)** — The official national employment service run by the Danish government, built specifically for international job seekers. It hosts 2,000–3,000 English-language vacancies at any given time, and most roles do not require Danish fluency.

**[New to Denmark](https://www.nyidanmark.dk/)** — The immigration authority’s official job and visa portal. It publishes the Positive List — a government-maintained list of shortage occupations updated every January and July. If your role is on this list, your work permit approval is significantly faster.

**[JobsInEnglish](https://jobsinenglish.dk/jobs-in-denmark-for-foreigners)** — Every single listing on this portal is posted in English, which is a direct signal that the employer is open to hiring internationally. Updated weekly with hundreds of new roles across Sales, Engineering, Software, and R&D.

**[Jobindex](https://www.jobindex.dk/)** — Denmark’s largest job portal, hosting roughly 80% of all job postings in the country. Founded in 1996. Most listings are in Danish, but filtering by English gives solid results.

**[State of Denmark](https://careers.state-of-denmark.com/)** — A recently launched portal where all jobs are hand-selected for international candidates. You can set up job alerts to stay updated.

**[EURES Portal](https://eures.europa.eu/)** — The EU’s official cross-border job mobility network with over 3 million vacancies across Europe, including 1,500+ Denmark-specific listings in Danish and English.

**[Life in Denmark](https://lifeindenmark.borger.dk/working/finding-a-job/finding-and-taking-a-job)** — The English-language version of Denmark’s public portal. Great for understanding work permits, regulated professions, and what documents you need before applying.

**[Career Denmark](https://careerdenmark.dk/product/job-in-denmark-list-of-companies-hiring-internationals/)** — A paid but highly curated database of 650+ verified companies in Denmark that have previously hired international profiles. Organized by city (Copenhagen, Aarhus, Odense, Aalborg) and by industry. Each entry links directly to the company’s careers page or LinkedIn.

**[Relocate.me](https://relocate.me/companies-hiring/denmark)** — Focuses specifically on tech companies in Denmark that hire internationally and offer relocation packages. A strong resource if you’re in software, data, or engineering.

**🏢 Companies in Denmark Known for Hiring Immigrants**

These companies operate globally, use English as a working language, and have a track record of hiring international talent across all seniority levels.

**Pharma & Life Sciences**

- [Novo Nordisk](https://www.novonordisk.com/careers.html) — Denmark’s largest company and a global diabetes/pharma leader. One of the most international workforces in the country.
- [Lundbeck](https://careers.lundbeck.com/) — Neuroscience pharmaceuticals, strong English-language culture.
- [Coloplast](https://www.coloplast.com/about-coloplast/careers/) — Medical devices, global operations from Copenhagen.
- [Chr. Hansen](https://www.chr-hansen.com/en/careers) — Bioscience and food ingredients, actively recruits internationally.

**Energy & Engineering**

- [Ørsted](https://orsted.com/en/careers) — The world’s largest offshore wind developer. Heavy demand for engineers, project managers, and analysts.
- [Vestas](https://www.vestas.com/en/careers) — Global wind turbine manufacturer. Strong international hiring across R&D and manufacturing.
- [Danfoss](https://www.danfoss.com/en/careers/) — Automation and climate solutions. Sponsors foreign workers, especially in engineering.
- [Grundfos](https://www.grundfos.com/careers) — Pump and water solutions. Known for hiring skilled trades and engineers internationally.

**Shipping & Logistics**

- [A.P. Moller-Maersk](https://www.maersk.com/careers) — One of the world’s largest shipping companies. Extremely international workforce across tech, logistics, and finance roles.

**Finance & Banking**

- [Danske Bank](https://danskebank.com/careers) — Denmark’s largest bank, strong English environment in its Copenhagen HQ.
- [Nordea](https://www.nordea.com/en/careers) — Largest bank across the Nordics. Tech and finance roles regularly open to internationals.

**Tech & IT**

- [KMD](https://www.kmd.dk/karriere) — Major Danish IT consultancy serving both private and public sector clients.

**Consumer & Lifestyle**

- [LEGO Group](https://www.lego.com/en-us/careers) — One of the most internationally recognized employers in Denmark. Offices in Billund and Copenhagen with global hiring.
- [Pandora](https://careers.pandoragroup.com/) — Global jewellery brand, Copenhagen HQ with strong international team.
- [Carlsberg](https://carlsberggroup.com/careers/) — One of the world’s top brewers, global operations run out of Copenhagen.

**Other Notable Employers**

- [ISS](https://www.issworld.com/en/careers) — Global facility services company headquartered in Copenhagen.
- [Velux](https://careers.velux.com/) — Roof windows and skylights, strong engineering and sales hiring.
- [Oticon](https://www.oticon.com/careers) — Hearing aid manufacturer — most roles are English-speaking.
- [FLSmidth](https://www.flsmidth.com/en-gb/careers) — Mining and cement engineering, active international hiring.
