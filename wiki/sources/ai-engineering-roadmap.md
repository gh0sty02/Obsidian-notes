# AI Engineering Roadmap — 12 Weeks to Production

**Origin:** Comprehensive 12-week AI engineering curriculum (authored by Claude for Pranay, March 2026)
**Target profile:** Senior SWE (3+ yrs, React/TS/Node) pivoting to AI Engineering, targeting Staff/Principal roles

---

## Phase Overview

| Phase | Weeks | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Foundations | 1–3 | LLM fundamentals, prompt engineering, embeddings |
| RAG Mastery | 4–7 | Vector DBs, RAG, evaluation |

---

## Hot Takes from the Roadmap

- **RAG > Fine-tuning**: 90% of companies use RAG first
- **LangChain is optional**: Learn vanilla first, framework second
- **Agents are the future**: Prioritize after RAG mastery
- **Evaluation is underrated**: Most engineers skip it; don't
- **Build in public**: Tweet progress, write about what you build
